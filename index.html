Zerui Chen 陈泽睿

Ph.D. student, Inria

I am a Ph.D. student at Inria and DI ENS, where I work with Cordelia Schmid, Shizhe Chen and Ivan Laptev on computer vision and robotics. Before my Ph.D., I obtained my master degree from Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, working with Liang Wang and Yan Huang. I received my bachelor degree from Northwestern Polytechnical University.
